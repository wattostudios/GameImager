How Do You Use Game Imager

Back to Index

Open an image from a game?

Click the "Open Image" button at the top of the screen - the SidePanel changes to show a list of files and folders.
Locate the file you want to open, and double-click it.
Game Imager will look at the image, and determine if a plugin can open it. If so, the image will be opened in the ImageList for you.


Open Image


Save an image, and use it in the game?

Click the "Save Image" button at the top of the screen, or at the bottom of the SidePanel.
Supply the name of your new image, choose the image format, and click the Save Collection button.
A progress bar is displayed to indicate the length of time left before the image will be fully saved.


Save Collection


Convert many images into a different format

Open as many images as you want, and notice that they will all appear in the ThumbnailList on the left.
Click the "Save Image" button at the top of the screen, or at the bottom of the SidePanel.
Choose the new format for the images, and click the Save Separate Images button.
A progress bar is displayed to indicate the length of time left before all the images will be fully converted.
